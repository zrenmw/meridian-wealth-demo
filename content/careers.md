At Meridian Wealth Advisors, we believe our people are our greatest asset. We foster a collaborative, intellectually stimulating environment where talented professionals can grow and make a meaningful impact on our clients' financial lives.

## Why Meridian?

- **Client-first culture**: Everything we do is guided by our fiduciary commitment
- **Professional development**: We sponsor certifications (CFP®, CFA®, CPA) and provide ongoing education
- **Work-life balance**: Competitive benefits, flexible scheduling, and generous PTO
- **Collaborative environment**: Open-door culture with mentorship from senior advisors
- **Growth opportunity**: We're growing thoughtfully, creating new roles and advancement paths
